Tiny House Meets RV Park

One way to describe us might be; “Tiny house meets RV Park“. Our property includes a security gate, video surveillance, and robust outdoor lighting for that extra peace of mind and added safety. We provide the perfect solution for college students, grandparents visiting adult children, and patients receiving medical care at St Luke’s, Methodist, Memorial Hermann, or Conroe Regional Medical Center. If you’re a contract worker or a single parent needing an affordable home close to your kids, we may be the perfect fit. Apartments and hotel rooms simply don’t provide the community and sanctuary of home you deserve. Apartments are unfurnished and have inflexible terms, while Hotel rooms are too confining and expensive.
